Project Description

I'd Like to draw some leaves falling from a tree. There are mainly 3 part of the animation.

1st part is the trunk part, which basically is a giant stick on the floor holding a bunch of solid spring. Under the wind force, these brunches will swing back and force. On the end of each brunches, there will be a leaf generator.

2nd part is the animation of those leaves falling down to the ground. This part is mainly come from the particle systems. I will try my best to add a spin force for it.

3rd part is the landing animation of each leaf. In my imagination, I will give those leaves rigid body feature. Those leaves may looks like a thin cube. And then fell on the ground. until its life ends,
